Type it that Way

The bachelor thesis is the result of a research about Hebrew typography and multi-script typesetting.

Type

Bachelor Thesis

Disciplines

Research, typography, multi-script typesetting

Tools

InDesign, Illustrator

Supervisors

Christian Stindl, Michael Pichler

The book is printed with a Pantone spot colour and handbound.

The anatomy of Hebrew type.

An overview of the characters in different languages and their relation.

The book includes a set of minimalistic outline infographics.

The headlines are set in English and Latin, exploiting the moment of different writing directions and flipping the page.
