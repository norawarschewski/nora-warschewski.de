Feel it that Way

My current research deals with the large topic of accessibility in design with a focus on typography in different media.

I’m investigating the various requirements and challenges when designing with accessibility in mind and as a priority. I believe, designing for accessibility calls for empathy and a change of perspectives to create successful products — in print, digital, and spatial designs.
