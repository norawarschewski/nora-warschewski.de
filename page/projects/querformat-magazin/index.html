Querformat Magazin

Querformat provides a queer perspective on contemporary phenomena, international art and pop culture. This intersectional perspective is also reflected in the design of the magazine with a strong emphasis on typography. Scientific texts and visual design are equally relevant for Querformat and each issue reinvents itself visually in accordance with the topic.

The presented issue is on “Hair Politics”.

All articles open with a large headline, followed by body text and imagery in a single column.

Image references get displayed by clicking on the reference number in the body text. They oben in the margin columns and remain open. The user can decide to be send down to the reference list or keep scrolling.

The navigation lists all relevant items. The latest issue is always slightly distanced out at the top.

The table of contents lists all articles including author and title.
