Pattern Generator

Patterns are the foundation of many design systems. They not only ease the work with the system but also help to easily and flexibly adjust the visual to different formats and derive assets.

With slight modifications in the code, the output can already change drastically and therefore making it very flexible in use for various projects.

The generator was creaated during a creative coding workshop conducted by Sander Sturing (Studio Dumbar).

Initial generated pattern as the foundation for further sketches.

Iteration with different shapes, grid size, and variation (used in my master thesis).

Generative typeface based on a mono-spaced pixel typeface as input (used in my master thesis).
