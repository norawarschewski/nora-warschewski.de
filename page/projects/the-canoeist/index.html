The Canoeist

The Canoeist is a magazine that deals with a variety of topics connected to canoeing, outdoor activities and nature with a focus on popular paddling destinations worldwide.

Deliverables included cover designs including one special edition, a sales animation, and media kit.

Special issues contain illustrations instead of photos and are therefore easily recognizable.

The media kit provides a short insight into facts and figures, topics, target group, overview of issues, and the editorial calendar.
