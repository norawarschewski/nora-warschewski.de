My name is Nora Warschewski and I’m currently working as a brand designer at Fraunhofer UMSICHT. I’m based in the Ruhr metropolis in Germany but I enjoy working from anywhere in the world.

I enjoy research-driven approaches and creating systems to work with. Besides, I’m interested in all sorts of topics evolving around type, its history and appearance in different media and contexts.

In my free-time, I love reading books, collecting stamps, running a small family archive, teaching myself stuff, and being outdoors.
